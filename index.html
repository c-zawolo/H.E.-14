WELCOME TO THE SPECIAL STAGE!

Team Sonic has been Trapped in the "Special Stage" by Eggman! Help them grab as many rings as they can by clicking the canvas! Use the buttons to place mini versions of Sonic and his friends to collect the rings! Use the L and R key to summon bumpers to ward of Eggman! When you've accumulated 50 rings, save a photo to collect your Chaos Emerald!
